## The Solution (30 seconds)

**ATLAS** is an autonomous agent that connects our existing tools into a single pipeline engine.

It does 5 things — automatically, every day:

| | What | How |
|-|------|-----|
| **1** | **Finds** who's ready to buy | Pulls intent signals from 6sense — surfaces accounts actively researching GRC solutions right now |
| **2** | **Researches** each account in seconds | AI generates a structured account brief in <60 seconds (replaces 15-30 min of manual research per account) |
| **3** | **Writes** personalized outreach | AI creates emails tailored to the buyer's role, their specific pain point, and a real trigger — not generic templates |
| **4** | **Recovers** dead pipeline | Automatically re-engages $5.5M in closed-lost deals with the right message at the right time (contract expiry, budget reset, product update) |
| **5** | **Monitors** pipeline health 24/7 | Alerts reps when deals go stale, flags at-risk pipeline, and delivers automated weekly reports to leadership |

**A human reviews and approves every outreach batch before it sends.** The agent does the work. The human makes the call.

## How It Works (The Short Version)

```
  6:00 AM    Agent scans 6sense for high-intent accounts
             Agent checks Salesforce for stale leads and dying deals
             
  6:05 AM    Agent researches each account (AI brief in <60 seconds)
             Agent writes personalized outreach per buyer persona
             
  9:00 AM    Nader reviews the batch (~15 minutes)
             Approves, edits, or rejects each email
             
  9:15 AM    Approved emails go out via Outreach
             Agent logs all activity to Salesforce
             
  All day    Agent monitors for replies every 30 minutes
             Hot replies → instant Teams alert to the assigned rep
             
  6:00 PM    Daily summary sent to leadership
```

**Total human time per day: 15-20 minutes.**
**Total accounts worked per month: 160+ (up from ~55).**
**Everything else is autonomous.**
